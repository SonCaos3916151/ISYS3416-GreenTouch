Our Mission Statement

Just as the first three industrial revolutions utilized steam engines, electricity and information technology to change global economies and transform agriculture, the upcoming fourth evolution, the digital revolution, will drastically reshape the infrastructure and scale of the agriculture industry. Our mission is to help farmers, who vital work has fed the world, to jump on this metaphorical bandwagon and lessen their burdens by giving them a digitized helper that is easy, reliable and secured to interact with.

How do I know it is Organic?

All pre-packaged organic food products in Europe which meet organic standards carry the Euro-Leaf EU organic logo.
