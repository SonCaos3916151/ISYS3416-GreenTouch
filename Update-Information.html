Farming automation

How technology facillitates farming.

 Farming automation is the process of utilizing machinary, sensors, electronics, and data management to lessen the need for human labor. The presence of farming automation systems have been met with, for the most part, positivity.
  Farming automation makes use of the following technologies:
         - Harvest automation
         - Autonomous automation
         - Seeding and Weeding
